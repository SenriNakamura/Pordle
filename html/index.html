How To Play
Guess the correct teacher's name in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid name.
  • The color of the tiles will change and shows how close your guess was to the name.
  • The number of the tiles in a row is same as the number of letters in the answer.
Examples
J is in the word and in the correct spot.
R is in the word but in the wrong spot.
N is not in the word in any spot.
